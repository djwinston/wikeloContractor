## Where to find it

Carinite is an ore recovered at **Align & Mine** sites. A rarer variant, *Carinite (Pure)*, drops
from the same locations.

## Step by step

1. Travel to an **Align & Mine** location.
2. Mine the deposits on site.
3. Haul the ore back and hand it in at Wikelo's Emporium.

## Notes

- Wikelo trades a **Wikelo Favor** for 50x Carinite, so a surplus is never wasted.
- Commodity prices and locations are tracked at [sc-trade.tools](https://sc-trade.tools/).
